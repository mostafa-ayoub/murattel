## Full Description (English)
Murattel - A free app for listening to the Holy Quran recited by the most renowned reciters in the Islamic world.

✨ Features:
• All 114 Surahs of the Holy Quran
• 42+ famous Quran reciters
• Real-time verse highlighting during playback
• Beautiful Arabic RTL interface
• Auto-play next surah
• Repeat and shuffle modes
• Search for surahs and reciters
• Playback progress control
• Dark elegant design

Designed by Moustafa Ayoub
